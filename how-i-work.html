How do I work

What can you expect when we engage on a project together

While each project may have individual nuances to adapt to, my core methodology starts with fully understanding the commercial and other relevant business goals, followed by the analysis of available resources, requirements, ideas and product roadmaps. It's important, that the key project stakeholders make themselves reasonably available during this phase. I produce a project plan to confirm sufficient mutual understanding of the deliverables, time and cost estimates. Depending of the nature of the project, I move on to the execution pahse and frequently report on the progress and collect feedback, until the project's completion.

During the execution phase, I can work with your internal / pre-selected teams, build a team according to the project needs or outsource your project to my network of qualified and tested professionals.
